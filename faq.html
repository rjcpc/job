FAQ

How do I create an employer account?

Quickly post job openings and manage your online recruiting efforts with ease by setting up an account with us today! It’s easy, if you are an employer, just use our website to get started.

How can we submit an ad for posting on JOB RADAR?

First, you must create an employer account on JOB RADAR. (if you don’t currently have one, it’s easy to set up, just use our website to get started. After you have created an account, you can quickly post job openings and manage your online recruiting efforts.

Why should our government agency advertise on JOB RADAR?

With almost 100,000 local government websites in the USA, it is impossible for job seekers to search all these sites to find job listings. JOB RADAR is a niche website for the professional government employee seeking employment. Currently, over 800 other websites link to our site providing even greater exposure to an agency’s job posting(s). Remember, no person can search 100,000 websites looking for employment.

When are ads posted?

Ads are posted daily within 24 hours of receipt except on weekends and Federal holidays.

How long is an ad posted?

An ad remains posted for 60 days. Ads are posted daily (Monday – Friday) excluding holidays.

What information should be included in an ad?

An ad should include the following: (1) Position Title; (2) Agency Name; (3) Salary and Benefits; (4) Job Description/Responsibilities; (5) Minimum Qualifications; (6) Other Pertinent Information to Applicant; (7) Closing Date for Application or Apply Immediately; (8) Information such as Contact Person, Name of Agency, Address, Telephone Number, FAX Number, E-mail Address, etc.; and (9) Affirmative Action or Equal Opportunity Information.

How do we pay for posting an ad on JOB RADAR?

Invoices will be sent and/or credit cards will be charged when ad/ads are placed on the JOB RADAR website. We accept Mastercard, Visa, Discover and American Express credit cards.

How many job postings are on our website at any given time?

The number of postings varies. Generally, we have between 300 and 900 postings at any time in the various Job Categories. Our job postings are easily accessible to potential applicants and we require no registration or password to access these postings.

Do we list job postings geographically?

Our service does not provide job postings by geographic areas. However, if you want a listing by state, just enter the state name in our “Search All Job Listing” box to access a state by state listing. Otherwise, all jobs are posted in specific “Job Categories.”

What hours are we open for business and/or to post ads on the website?

Monday through Friday, 6:00 am – 6:00 pm, Central Standard Time (CST), except for Federal holidays. For support, please call 1-888-575-9675.

Who is JOB RADAR?

JOB RADAR is the leading provider of website job postings for city, county and state governments and agencies. We have been in business since 1997 and have nationwide exposure. Our niche is serving governments nationwide advertising their job searches. We are a privately owned company.

How do I create a job seeker account?

If you are a job seeker searching for government jobs, to create a job seeker account for free, use our website.

Can I post my resume on JOB RADAR?

Yes, you can post your resume for free. To post your resume for free, use our website.

How many job postings are on our website at any given time?

The number of postings varies. Generally, we have between 150 and 250 postings at any time in the various Job Categories. Our job postings are easily accessible to potential applicants and we require no registration or password to access these postings.

Can I get additional information about the job posting other than the information listed?

The information provided is given to us by the employer. However, additional information about the job postings can be obtained from the government agency by contacting them directly.

To whom should application materials be sent?

All resumes and applications should be sent to the government posting the ad. Just follow instructions given in the job posting. Do not send application materials to JOB RADAR. We are NOT a placement agency.
