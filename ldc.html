Lower Division Clerk

The work or job role of a Lower Division Clerk is simple, and they are usually required to perform Simple tasks in the allotted offices. Selected candidates against LDC are required to below tasks, Enter the date into the computer (Data Entry work). Job role includes maintenance of required files and documents. Making the salary of the office staff. Sending or receiving the important files/ documents. He/ she needs to register leave of the office staff. Must be able to type the letter and sometimes required to send the same through FAX or Post.
Eligibility : 10th pass/ITI
